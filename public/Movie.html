My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Avatar Series

This film series takes place on an alien planet and focuses the brutality of capitalist human society vs the more community centeres life of the natives to Pandora. The visual and vfx effects are absolutely striking.

The Matrix Series

This is one of my favourite set of movies and has held its own with an intriguing story and captiviating action scenes. An all time classic.

Rocky

For all the fighters out there Rocky is a classic film that follows the rise of a failed boxer 'Rocky Balboa' through his struglle and training for the match of his life.
